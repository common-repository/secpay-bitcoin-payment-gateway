Enables you to accept Bitcoin payments with the service of SecPay (https://www.secpay.io) in your woocommerce store.

== Description ==

The plugin depends on the API of the payment provider SecPay (https://www.secpay.io).
After the onboarding you'll be able to accept Bitcoin payments in your store, while the payout will be automated in EUR or CHF
to your SEPA bank account.

To use this plugin you need a secpay.io account. For testing purposes you can request that for free at tobias.zander@secpay.io.
For production please contact info@secpay.io to provide you the terms and credentials.
